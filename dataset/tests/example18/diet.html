Greek Chickpea Salad

Prep 15mServes 4~350 kcalP20/C35/F14g

Ingredients

  • 2 cans chickpeas (rinsed)
  • 1 cucumber, 1 bell pepper, 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • ½ red onion, ½ cup Kalamata olives
  • ¼ cup feta (optional)
  • Dressing: 3 tbsp olive oil, 1 tbsp red wine vinegar, oregano, salt

Steps

  1. Chop veg; combine with chickpeas & olives.
  2. Whisk dressing, toss, top with feta.
  3. Chill 10 minutes, serve.

Swap feta → vegan feta for dairy‑free.

Herb‑Crusted Salmon & Quinoa

Total 25mServes 2~520 kcalP38/C42/F22g

Ingredients

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • Herb mix (dill, parsley), 2 tsp mustard
  • 1 lemon, olive oil, salt, pepper

Steps

  1. Rub salmon with mustard, herbs, salt, pepper.
  2. Pan‑sear 3–4m/side; rest.
  3. Toss quinoa with lemon & oil; plate with salmon.

Add steamed greens for fiber.

Keto

Very low carb; emphasize fats and moderate protein.

Garlic‑Butter Chicken Thighs

Total 30mServes 3~560 kcalP35/C5/F42g

Ingredients

  • 6 boneless chicken thighs
  • 3 tbsp butter, 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 tsp paprika, salt, pepper
  • ½ lemon, chopped parsley

Steps

  1. Sear thighs skin‑side down 6–7m; flip 5–6m.
  2. Add butter & garlic; baste.
  3. Finish with lemon & parsley.

Serve with sautéed zucchini “noodles”.

Cauliflower “Risotto”

Total 20mServes 2~320 kcalP12/C10/F24g

Ingredients

  • 3 cups riced cauliflower
  • 2 tbsp cream cheese or coconut cream
  • ¼ cup grated parmesan (optional)
  • Butter, garlic, pepper

Steps

  1. Sauté garlic in butter; add cauliflower rice.
  2. Stir 5–7m; fold in cream cheese & parmesan.
  3. Season to taste; serve creamy.

For dairy‑free, use coconut cream + nutritional yeast.

Vegan

All plant‑based; mindful of B12, iron, complete proteins.

Lentil & Sweet Potato Curry

Total 35mServes 4~420 kcalP18/C60/F10g

Ingredients

  • 1 cup red lentils (rinsed)
  • 1 sweet potato (diced)
  • 1 onion, 2 cloves garlic, 1 tbsp curry paste
  • 1 can coconut milk, 2 cups veg stock
  • Spinach, lime

Steps

  1. Sauté onion & garlic; stir curry paste.
  2. Add lentils, sweet potato, stock; simmer 15–18m.
  3. Stir in coconut milk & spinach; season; lime to finish.

Tofu & Broccoli Stir‑Fry

Total 20mServes 2~380 kcalP25/C30/F16g

Ingredients

  • 14 oz firm tofu (pressed, cubed)
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • Sauce: 2 tbsp soy/tamari, 1 tbsp maple, 1 tsp sesame oil, ginger, garlic
  • Cornstarch (for tofu toss)

Steps

  1. Toss tofu with cornstarch; pan‑sear until golden.
  2. Stir‑fry broccoli 3–4m; add sauce & tofu; coat.
  3. Serve with brown rice or quinoa.

Gluten‑Free

Avoid wheat/barley/rye; verify certified GF ingredients.

Chicken & Rice Burrito Bowl

Total 30mServes 3~520 kcalP35/C60/F14g

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (or cauliflower rice)
  • 2 chicken breasts (grilled, sliced)
  • Black beans, corn, pico de gallo
  • Avocado, lime, cilantro

Steps

  1. Layer rice, beans, corn.
  2. Top with chicken, pico, avocado.
  3. Lime & cilantro to finish.

Use GF‑certified stock & seasonings.

Garlic Shrimp Polenta

Total 25mServes 2~480 kcalP32/C45/F18g

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup quick‑cook polenta
  • 10–12 shrimp, peeled
  • 2 tbsp butter or olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, chili flakes, lemon
  • Parmesan (optional), parsley

Steps

  1. Cook polenta per pack; season; optional parmesan.
  2. Sauté garlic & shrimp 2–3m until pink.
  3. Plate polenta; top with shrimp; lemon, parsley.

High‑Protein

Meals emphasizing ≥25–35g protein per serving.

Turkey Chili (One‑Pot)

Total 40mServes 4~480 kcalP38/C42/F16g

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground turkey
  • 1 onion, 1 bell pepper
  • 1 can diced tomatoes, 1 can kidney beans
  • 2 tbsp chili powder, cumin, salt
  • Stock as needed

Steps

  1. Sauté onion/pepper; brown turkey.
  2. Add spices, tomatoes, beans; simmer 20m.
  3. Adjust seasoning; serve with yogurt (optional).

Greek Yogurt Chicken Salad Wraps

Total 15mServes 2~420 kcalP35/C35/F14g

Ingredients

  • 2 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • ½ cup Greek yogurt, 1 tsp dijon
  • Celery, apple, dill, lemon juice
  • Whole‑grain wraps or lettuce cups

Steps

  1. Mix yogurt, dijon, lemon; fold in chicken & veg.
  2. Season; fill wraps or lettuce cups.
  3. Serve with greens.
